常见问题解答 (FAQ)

1. 如何在不同设备之间同步我的习惯数据？
小习惯支持通过 Apple iCloud 进行云端同步。请确保您的所有 iOS 设备登录了相同的 Apple ID，并在应用底部的“设置”->“iCloud 同步”中开启同步开关。开启后，系统将在后台自动加密同步数据。
2. 桌面小组件 (Widget) 为什么未能直接刷新打卡状态？
iOS 系统为了保护续航，对小组件的后台刷新频率有限制。如果您在小组件上完成打卡，小组件会立即做出响应。如果在主应用中调整了数据，小组件通常会在数秒到数分钟内同步；您也可以重新打开一次应用，系统会立刻强制全量刷新所有桌面小组件。
3. 更换手机或重装应用后，如何恢复已购买的会员？
进入应用内的“设置”或“购买会员”页面，点击右上角或底部的“恢复购买 (Restore Purchases)”按钮。只要您的设备当前登录的 Apple ID 是此前购买时使用的账号，系统就会通过 Apple StoreKit 安全验证并直接为您恢复高级会员权益，无需重复购买。
4. 如何手动备份我长年累月的打卡记录？
请在“设置”页面中点击“导出数据 (CSV)”，您可以在弹出的系统菜单中将所有的打卡记录表格保存到“文件”App、发送到电脑或微信备份，确保您的习惯资产永久留存。

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I sync my habit data across multiple Apple devices?
Little Habit Tracker supports seamless cloud sync via Apple iCloud. Ensure that all your iOS devices are signed into the same Apple ID, and enable the "iCloud Sync" toggle in the App's Settings tab. Your data will sync securely in the background.
2. Why is my Home Screen Widget not updating immediately?
To preserve battery life, iOS regulates background widget refresh rates. When you check in directly on the widget, it updates instantly. If you make changes inside the main app, widgets usually refresh within moments; opening the main app will also force an immediate sync of all widgets.
3. How do I restore my premium subscription or lifetime purchase?
Go to the App's Settings or Paywall screen and tap "Restore Purchases". As long as you are logged into the same Apple ID used for the original transaction, Apple StoreKit will securely verify and restore your premium access without double-charging you.
4. Can I export or backup my habit records locally?
Yes! In the Settings tab, tap "Export Data (CSV)". You can save your full check-in history to iOS Files, AirDrop it to your Mac, or send it via email for safe, long-term safekeeping.
